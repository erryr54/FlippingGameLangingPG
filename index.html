What is Retail Artitrage?

Retail Aritrage is when you buy a product from a store or brand and resell it at a higher price to profit from the difference. Retail Arbitrage is a form of reselling.

What is Reselling?
"A reseller is a person or company that buys products from different suppliers and sells them to their own customers (usually at a profit). Resellers often provide some value-add in terms of the customer experience, which enables them to mark up the price of the items they sell."
